# Customer Discovery Survey Questions

## Section A: Respondent Background
1. What best describes you at the university?
2. How do you usually access your class timetable today?

## Section B: The Problem (Past Experience)
3. When was the last time the printed or PDF timetable was wrong, outdated, or different from what actually happened in class?
4. What did you do when you discovered the mismatch?
5. How long, roughly, did it take to sort out the confusion?
6. What was the outcome that time? (e.g. arrived late, went to the wrong room, missed something important)

## Section C: Frequency and Severity
7. How often do you personally run into outdated or unclear schedule info?
8. What is the single most annoying part of relying on the current PDF timetable?

## Section D: Solution Validation
9. How much would a real-time digital timetable improve your day-to-day routine?
10. If a free digital version of the timetable existed with live updates, how likely are you to switch to it?
11. What is one feature that would make you trust a digital timetable over the current PDF?

## Section E: Open Feedback
12. Any suggestions?
